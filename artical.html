COMPUTER

### Computer **Definition:** A computer is an electronic device designed to process, store, and retrieve data. It operates through a set of instructions, known as programs, to perform various tasks. **Key Features:** 1. **Input:** Receives data through input devices (e.g., keyboard, mouse). 2. **Processing:** Performs calculations and manipulations using the CPU. 3. **Storage:** Saves data and programs on storage devices (e.g., hard drives, SSDs). 4. **Output:** Displays or produces results through output devices (e.g., monitor, printer). **Types of Computers:** - **Personal Computers (PCs):** For individual use, including desktops and laptops. - **Servers:** Provide resources and services to other computers over a network. - **Supercomputers:** High-performance systems for complex computations. - **Embedded Systems:** Specialized computers integrated into other devices. **Importance:** Computers are essential for various applications, including business, education, communication, and entertainment, shaping how we interact with the world.
